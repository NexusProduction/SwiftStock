Sign Up as Owner

Create manager, employee, or viewer accounts from your dashboard. Share credentials via WhatsApp, Gmail, or any app — in text or image.

Access Levels

The right permissions
for every role.

👑
Owner
  • Full platform access
  • Create & remove any staff
  • View all IDs & passwords
  • Delete products & entries
  • Receive all notifications
  • Manage all godowns
🛡️
Manager
  • Create & manage staff
  • Create & manage godowns
  • Add & delete products
  • View assigned godowns
  • Log IN/OUT entries
  • Cannot remove Owner
🧑‍💼
Staff / Viewer
  • View assigned godowns only
  • Add product entries
  • View own profile
  • No account management
  • No staff creation
  • Viewers: read only
