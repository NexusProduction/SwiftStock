How it works

From setup to live tracking
in under 2 minutes.

01 / 04
Create your account
Sign up as an Owner. Your account becomes the company hub — all godowns, staff, and data live under your company ID.
02 / 04
Add your godowns
Create warehouses with names and locations. Assign custom staff to each or give all staff access — your call.
03 / 04
Add products
Define products with units and optional unit conversions — like 1 box = 12 pieces. Stock numbers always stay accurate.
04 / 04
Log stock, live
Staff log IN and OUT entries from any phone. Every entry is timestamped, attributed, and synced instantly across all devices.

Roles

The right access for
every person.

SwiftStock has four clearly defined roles. Owners have full control. Managers run operations. Employees log entries. Viewers read only.

👑
Owner

Full control. Create and delete godowns, manage all staff, and access everything in the company.

  • Manage all godowns
  • Add & remove staff
  • Delete products & entries
  • View all notifications
  • Company-wide access
🛡️
Manager

Day-to-day operations. Manage assigned godowns, add staff, and oversee inventory movement.

  • Manage assigned godowns
  • Add & manage staff
  • Add & delete products
  • Log IN/OUT entries
  • Cannot remove Owner
👷
Employee / Viewer

On the ground. Log stock entries in assigned godowns. Viewers get read-only access with no edit permissions.

  • View assigned godowns
  • Log stock entries
  • View own profile
  • Viewers: read only
  • No admin access
